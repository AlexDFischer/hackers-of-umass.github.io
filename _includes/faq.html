Is this for me?

A few frequently asked questions

  • #1

    Who can join?

    All UMass students are welcome to join! Your major, level of experience, and year of graduation don't matter; we're just looking for fun, talented, and motivated people.

  • #2

    Great, how do I sign up?

    Thanks for asking! We're an ACM interest group, so join that club first; don't worry, there are no fees or obligations that come with being a member. Once you do that, scroll down the page for more info!

  • #3

    What if I don't know how to program?

    Then we want you to come to a meeting even more! Getting started is the first step to becoming an expert. In fact, you don't even have to program if you don't want to.

  • #4

    Okay, but where is ILC S311 located?

    Walk into the ILC from the North Pleasant Street entrance (where the buses stop), and go up the stairs and to the left to get to room S311.
