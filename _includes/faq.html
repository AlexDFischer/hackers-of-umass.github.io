Is this for me?

A few frequently asked questions

  • #1

    Who can join?

    All UMass students are welcome to join! Your major, level of experience, and year of graduation don't matter; we're just looking for fun, talented, and motivated people.

  • #2

    Great, how do I sign up?

    We're glad you asked! Head over to the members page for information. Since we're an ACM interest group, you'll have to join the UMass ACM chapter as well. Don't worry, there are no fees or obligations that come with being an ACM member!

  • #3

    What if don't know how to program?

    Then we want you to come to a meeting even more! Getting started is the first step to becoming an expert. In fact, you don't even have to program if you don't want to.

  • #4

    Okay, but where is this "U-Space" located?

    Walk in through the main enterance of the CS building, go through a few sets of double-doors and take a right. Then, walk until you've reached a set of double doors with a keypad lock. If you don't know the password, shoot us an email using the form below (and a UMass email address).
